TOI-4633 c: Automated Exoplanet Validation

Developed a Python-based pipeline using Box Least Squares (BLS) periodograms to detect and validate transits in TESS light curves. Successfully confirmed the 271.5-day orbit of TOI-4633 c in a complex binary star system.

Technical Stack: Python, Lightkurve, Matplotlib, NumPy.
